Ball Blast · Ember Strike

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Summary

Ball Blast: Ember Strike is a single-player game. If you choose to set a display name, we store that name along with your best score on Firebase servers so a global leaderboard can be shown. If you skip the display name, no personal data ever leaves your device.

What We Collect

When you set a display name and submit a score, we store:

• Display name (as you typed it)
• Highest score achieved on this device
• Timestamp of the submission
• Anonymous player ID — a random UUID generated on first launch, stored locally and used only to keep your leaderboard entry stable across sessions.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location, photos, real names, passwords, or payment information. There are no ads, no third-party advertising trackers, and no in-app purchases. Your anonymous player ID is not linked to your Apple ID or any device identifier like IDFA or IDFV.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (both provided by Google) to understand gameplay trends and diagnose crashes. These services may collect aggregated, de-identified information such as app version, device model, iOS version, and crash traces. No personally identifying information is collected. Google's handling of this data is described at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data Storage

Leaderboard data is stored in Google Firestore (US-based Google Cloud infrastructure). Your anonymous player ID and display name are the only fields associated with you, and only you can identify yourself from the public leaderboard (by recognizing your own name).

Children's Privacy

Ball Blast: Ember Strike does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game collects no data at all if you do not set a display name. If you believe a child has submitted a leaderboard entry and you would like it removed, email the contact below.

Your Choices

• Opt out completely: do not set a display name. No data ever leaves your device.
• Reset local data: Settings → Reset Stats clears all local progress, high scores, and achievements on this device.
• Delete your leaderboard entry: email duskinterteling@gmail.com with your display name. We'll remove it within 7 days.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top and post a notice in the app before the changes take effect.
